About Us

ANDI by Andile is a made to order slow fashion online store catering for young professionals, college students and high school students in Harare.
The store was developed by Andile Dube who is a college student with a high taste in and a lot of passion for fashion.
Andile has knowledge in technopreneurship, business management, garment construction and slow fashion.
She aims to design and make beautiful, trendy clothes that are functional, kind to the environment and still affordable.
